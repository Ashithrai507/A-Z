# Interview Template – "Introduce Yourself"

---

## 🔹 Basic Structure (Easy to Remember)

1. Greeting  
2. Name + Education  
3. Skills  
4. Projects / Experience  
5. Strengths  
6. Career Goal  
7. Closing Line  

---

##  Full Simple Template

Good morning sir/ma’am.

My name is Ashith Rai. I am currently pursuing B-tech in AI/ML from VCET.

I have strong interest in core fundamental concepts of computer science. I have learned skills like CN, OS, and game dev.

I have worked on projects such as ...... Through this project, I learned ......

My strengths are that I am ----, ---, and I like to keep learning new things.

My short-term goal is to ---. My long-term goal is to ----.

That’s a brief introduction about me. Thank you.

---

##  Short Version (For 30–40 Seconds)

Good morning sir/ma’am.

My name is ______. I am pursuing/completed ______ in ______.

I am interested in ______ and have skills in ______.

I have worked on ______ project which helped me improve my ______ skills.

I am a quick learner and a responsible person. I am looking for an opportunity where I can grow and contribute to the company.

Thank you.

---

##  Important Tips

✔ Keep it under 1 minute  
✔ Don’t tell your full life story  
✔ Focus on skills related to the job  
✔ Be confident and smile  
✔ Don’t memorize word by word — understand and speak naturally  

---

##  Simple Formula to Remember

Present → Past → Skills → Project → Goal → Close

(Who you are now → What you did → What you know → What you built → Where you want to go)
