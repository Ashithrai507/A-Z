# GD Blueprint


## 1️ Opening


- Good morning everyone. The topic given to us is ____. I would like to start by saying that this topic is very relevant today.
- In simple terms, this topic is about ____. Let me begin by sharing my thoughts.
- I would like to start the discussion by looking at both sides of this topic.
- I would like to begin by defining the topic first.
- Let us first understand what this topic really means.

---

## 2️ How to Agree

Always agree + add something new.

### Template:

- I agree with what you said.
- Yes, I also feel the same.
- I would like to support your point.


- Adding to that…
- I would also like to say that…
- Along with that…

### Example:

I agree with your point that social media helps in communication. Adding to that, it also helps in spreading awareness quickly.

---

## 3️ How to Disagree (Politely)

Never say: “You are wrong.”

### Template:

- I slightly disagree with your point.
- I see your point, but I have a different opinion.
- I understand what you are saying, however…
- I would like to look at it from another side.

### Example:

I understand your point that online education is convenient. However, I feel classroom learning gives better interaction.

---

## 4️ How to Appreciate

### Template:

- That’s a good point.
- You explained that very clearly.
- I really liked your example.
- That was an interesting perspective.

Then connect:

- Building on that…
- Related to that…

---

## 5️ How to Introduce a New Perspective

When discussion is stuck or repeating.

### Template:

- I would like to bring another angle to this discussion.
- Let’s also think about this from a different point of view.
- We have discussed the advantages, now let’s talk about the challenges.
- So far we spoke about students, what about working professionals?

---

## 6️ How to Interrupt Respectfully (If Needed)

### Template:

- Excuse me, may I add something here?
- Sorry to interrupt, but I would like to add a small point.
- Can I quickly add something?
- Just to add to that…

Keep your point short after interrupting.

---

## 7️ Short Carry-Forward Structure (Easy Flow)

###  1. Define  
###  2. Advantages  
###  3. Disadvantages  
###  4. Real Example  
###  5. Conclusion / Solution  

### Example Flow:

1. First, let us understand what this topic means.
2. There are some advantages like…
3. However, there are also some disadvantages such as…
4. For example…
5. So in conclusion, I feel…

---

##  30-Second Mini GD Template

The topic given to us is ____. In my opinion, this topic has both positive and negative sides. On one side ____. On the other side ____. So I believe we should focus on balancing both.

##  What Companies Check in GD

- Confidence  
- Clarity  
- Listening skills  
- Respect for others  
- Leadership  
- Not English fluency  

Simple English + clear thinking = success.
